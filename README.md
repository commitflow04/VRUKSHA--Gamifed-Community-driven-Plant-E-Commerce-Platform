# VRUKSHA

## Plant Growth Tracking

The [36mVRUKSHA[0m application provides a comprehensive platform for tracking the growth of plants. Users can monitor their plants over time using various features that include:

### Growth Tracking Features:
1. **Image Uploads**: Users can take pictures of their plants at different growth stages and upload them to the application.
2. **Growth Point Calculation**: The application utilizes advanced algorithms to calculate growth points based on uploaded images. These points reflect the health and growth rate of the plant, taking into account:
   - **Leaf Count**: More leaves generally indicate healthier and faster growth.
   - **Height Measurement**: The height of the plant is tracked over time to assess its growth.
   - **Health Indicators**: Analyzing the color and condition of the leaves helps determine the overall plant health.

## Technical Overview

### Image Processing
- The application uses image recognition technology to analyze the pictures of plants. It identifies key features like leaf count and color.
- The processed images feed into the growth point calculation algorithm, allowing users to visualize their plant growth journey.

### Growth Points Calculation
- Growth points are calculated using a combination of data from user-uploaded images and predefined growth standards. The algorithm considers:
  - The age of the plant
  - Environmental factors such as light and moisture
  - Historical growth data to provide accurate assessments.
